French Fries

Description

French fries (North American English), chips (British English), finger chips (Indian English), French-fried potatoes, or simply fries, are batonnet or allumette-cut deep-fried potatoes, originating from either Belgium or France. hey are prepared by cutting the potato into even strips, then drying and frying it, usually in a deep fryer. Most french fries are produced from frozen Russet potatoes.

French fries are served hot, either soft or crispy, and are generally eaten as part of lunch or dinner or by themselves as a snack, and they commonly appear on the menus of diners, fast food restaurants, pubs, and bars. They are often salted and may be served with ketchup, vinegar, mayonnaise, tomato sauce, or other local specialties. Fries can be topped more heavily, as in the dishes of poutine or chili cheese fries. Chips can be made from sweet potatoes instead of potatoes. A baked variant, oven chips, uses less or no oil.

Ingredients

  • 1 (28 ounce) package frozen French fries (such as Ore Ida®)
  • 3 slices bacon, sliced into 1/4-inch pieces
  • 1 tablespoon butter
  • ¼ cup minced scallions
  • 1 tablespoon minced garlic
  • ½ cup blue cheese crumbles
  • 2 tablespoons half-and-half

Recipe instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 450 degrees F (230 degrees C).
  2. Spread French fries in a single layer on a baking sheet.
  3. Bake in the preheated oven until lightly golden and crisp, 24 to 34 minutes.
  4. Place bacon in a large skillet and cook over medium-high heat; cook and stir until evenly browned, about 10 minutes.
  5. Drain bacon slices on paper towels, reserving some of the bacon grease in the skillet.
  6. Slice into 1/4-inch strips.
  7. Stir butter into the bacon grease and set skillet over medium heat; add scallions and garlic.
  8. Cook and stir until scallions are soft and browning, about 5 minutes.
  9. Add blue cheese; cook and stir with a spatula until cheese is melted, 2 to 3 minutes.
  10. Stir in half-and-half until sauce is smooth, 2 to 3 minutes. Remove from heat and let sit for 2 minutes.
  11. Arrange fries on a plate and drizzle sauce over fries. Sprinkle bacon strips on top.
